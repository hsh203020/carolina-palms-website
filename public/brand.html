Brand Usage Rules

Maintain brand integrity

Logo Usage

✓ DO

Use on dark navy or cream backgrounds. Maintain minimum clear space. Use provided SVG files.

✗ DON'T

Place on busy backgrounds. Stretch or distort. Add shadows or effects.

Color Application

✓ DO

Navy for backgrounds. Gold for accents/CTAs. Cream for light mode. Emerald for success.

✗ DON'T

Use gold as background. Mix non-palette colors. Use bright/neon tones.

Tone of Voice

✓ DO

Luxury, refined, aspirational. Professional yet warm. Bilingual AR/EN.

✗ DON'T

Casual/slang. Aggressive sales. "Cheap" or "discount" framing.
